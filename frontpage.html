Frequently Asked Questions

Sirmata.com is a dedicated online scholarship portal specifically designed for the students and incoming freshmen of the University of Caloocan City (UCC). It serves as a centralized repository where users can discover various financial grants and educational assistance programs tailored to their academic journey.

No, Sirmata.com does not act as the awarding body for the financial grants listed on the platform. The site functions as an information and navigation hub that displays available opportunities, while the actual evaluation and disbursement of funds remain with the respective scholarship providers..

Yes, all scholarship opportunities featured on the platform are verified for authenticity. The site ensures that the listings are legitimate to provide a secure and trustworthy environment for UCC students seeking financial aid.

The platform is entirely free of charge and requires no payment for access or usage. It is a public resource specifically maintained to support the student body and applicants of the University of Caloocan City without any financial barriers.

To begin an application, you must first register for an account and log in to the portal to access the full listings. Once you have identified a grant that matches your qualifications, you simply need to click the "Apply Here" button to proceed with the specific requirements for that scholarship.

Yes, the platform is equipped with an integrated filtering system that allows users to sort through listings based on their personal preferences. This functionality enables students to efficiently find the grants that best align with their specific academic standing or financial needs.
