What's Sirmata?

IRMATA is a website that provides information about scholarship opportunities in the University of Caloocan City. It serves as a centralized platform where students can easily access important details such as application deadlines, requirements, and eligibility criteria.

The website aims to make scholarship information more accessible and organized, helping students avoid confusion and missed opportunities. By gathering all necessary details in one place, SIRMATA simplifies the application process.

Meaning Behind our Name & Logo

The name “SIRMATA” comes from a dialect of Ilokano, meaning “vision.” It reflects the website’s purpose of guiding students toward better educational opportunities and helping them see a clearer path for their future.

The logo is inspired by a Baybayin character with the value “sa,” transformed into the letter “S.” This design connects cultural identity with a modern interpretation of the website’s name, symbolizing encouragement and academic success.
