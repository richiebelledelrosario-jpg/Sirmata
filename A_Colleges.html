Philippine National Police (PNP) Scholarship / Support Programs

APPLY HERE

Eligibility

  • Filipino citizen
  • Must be a student of Criminal Justice, Criminology, or related fields
  • moral character

Requirements

  • Application form (contact PNP Education & Training Branch or local PNP station)
  • Police Clearance
  • Report card or Transcript of Records (TOR)
  • Recommendation / endorsement from local police unit or barangay

Campus

  • South
  • Camarin

Benefits

  • Partial or full tuition fee assistance
  • Allowance for books and school supplies
  • Uniform or miscellaneous fee support
  • Possible monthly stipend (varies per branch)

Deadline

  • Dependent on the PNP office issuing the scholarship: check with your local PNP station or PNP headquarters

Department of Education (DepEd) – Teacher Education Scholarship Support

APPLY HERE

Eligibility

  • Filipino citizen
  • Enrolled in Teacher Education programs (BSED / BEED / Early Childhood Education)
  • Good academic standing
  • Willing to serve in Philippine schools after graduation (for some programs)

Requirements

  • Proof of enrollment
  • Recommendation letter
  • Report card or Transcript of Records (TOR)
  • Application form

Campus

  • South
  • Camarin
  • Congress
  • Bagong Silang

Benefits

  • Tuition and school fee assistance (for private institutions)
  • For public universities like UCC: financial assistance given as stipend
  • ₱3,000 – ₱8,000 monthly living allowance (depending on budget allocation)
    • OR
  • ₱10,000 – ₱20,000 per semester financial grant
  • ₱3,000 – ₱5,000 annual book allowance
  • Practice Teaching / Internship support
  • Possible priority hiring in public schools after graduation
  • Professional development training and seminars
  • May require 1–3 years return service in DepEd public schools

Deadline

  • Varies per program announcement
  • Usually announced before start of academic year (based on previous cycles)

Department of Science and Technology – DOST-SEI Scholarship (For BSED Science)

APPLY HERE

Eligibility

  • Filipino citizen
  • STEM SHS graduate (for freshmen)
  • Enrolled in BSED Science or other science-related programs
  • Strong grades in Math & Science

Requirements

  • Online application
  • SHS report card or TOR
  • PSA Birth Certificate
  • Examination (if required)

Campus

  • South
  • Camarin
  • Congress
  • Bagong Silang

Benefits

  • ₱8,000 monthly living allowance
  • ₱40,000 per academic year tuition support (for private HEIs)
  • Book allowance: ₱10,000 per year
  • Clothing allowance: ₱1,000 (1st sem, 1st year only)
  • Thesis allowance: ₱10,000
  • Graduation allowance: ₱1,000
  • Group accident insurance
    • (For public HEIs like UCC, tuition is usually covered under the ₱40,000 cap but since UCC is free tuition, allowance is the main benefit.)

Deadline

  • Usually August–October yearly

  • PSA Birth Certificate
