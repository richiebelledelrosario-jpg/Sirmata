Scholarship Type

  • Merit-Based
    • These awards recognize students with outstanding academic records or special talents.
    They provide financial support to help high-achievers reach their full potential.
  • Needs-Based
    • This aid is designed for students who require financial assistance to cover their education.
    It ensures that every student has a fair chance to study regardless of their budget.
  • Government Funded
    • These programs use public funds to make schooling more affordable for citizens.
    They represent a community investment in the future of the country’s workforce.
  • Private Sponsorship
    • These are grants provided by companies or individuals to support a student's career goals.
    They often open doors for future employment and professional networking.
